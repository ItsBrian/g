Step 1: Creating the channel

You could do this in a channel that already exists but this channel will get spammed with messages if you keep it running

Step 2: Creating the Webhook

Hover over your channel and click on "Edit Chanel"

Locate the "Webhooks" tab in the channel settings

Click on "Create Webhook"

Create the webhook

Note: When creating the webhook you don't need to add an icon because the generator will do this anyway

You're done!
Just copy your webhook URL/link, paste it in the generator and click start!
